CAVE

Conversational Architecture Visual Expression language

Hello. We're currently developing the first iteration of CAVE, the Conversational Architecture Visual Expression language. The goal of CAVE is to allow product development teams describe contextual applications.

We believe that natural language conversations are the best metaphor to articulate the functionality of a contextual application.

We are currently developing an initial specification for CAVE, and we want your feedback on it. If you'd like to see it when it's published, please enter your email address in the form below and we'll let you know when it's ready.
